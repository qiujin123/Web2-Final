LEAGUE OF LEGENDS WORLD CHAMPIONSHIP 2019

ABOUT

The 2019 League of Legends World Championship is the ninth world championship for League of Legends, an esports tournament for the video game developed by Riot Games. The two team that made it into the final are Funplus Phoenix representing China and G2 Esport representing Europe. Who will be the winner of the 2019 League of Legends World Championship? Come and watch the live battle between the two team, or watch it online from our website, twitch.tv and youtube.

LOCATION

  • Date: November 10 12:00 UTC (Game 1 at 13:00 UTC)
  • Venue: AccorHotels Arena, Paris, France
  • Address: 8 Boulevard de Bercy, 75012 Paris, France

OPENING CEREMONY

In 2017, Adam Mackasek helped summon a dragon in Beijing. During the annual League of Legends World Championships at the 80,000-seat Beijing National Stadium. But this year we are going even bigger on our stage setup. Powered by a technology called 3D Holonet, created by a company called Kaleida. The Holonet allows us to do different types of effects that weren’t necessarily possible with augmented reality technology. This year the 3 sound are going to be “Giants” by True Damage, featuring Becky G, Soyeon, Thutmose, Duckwrth and Keke Palmer. “Awaken” by Valerie Broussard and the 2019 World song “Phoenix” by Cailin Russo and Chrissy Costanza.
